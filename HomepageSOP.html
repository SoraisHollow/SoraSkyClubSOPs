Welcome to the SoraSkyClub staff hub. This page explains what we look for in our team and links you to the SOPs for each core role: Hosts, Bouncers, and Admins.

What We’re Looking For in Staff

Every SoraSkyClub staff member represents the brand, the community, and the vibe of our worlds. No matter your role, we expect:

  • Reliability: Show up on time, follow through on what you say you’ll do.
  • Clear Communication: Use the planning and staff channels, log what happened, ask when unsure.
  • Professionalism: Friendly, calm, and respectful—even when someone else isn’t.
  • Consistent Logging: Events and infractions should always be recorded in Notion.
  • Team Mindset: You are never “alone on duty.” Keep admins and other staff in the loop.
  • Comfort & Safety: Our spaces should feel safe, inclusive, and welcoming.
  • Alignment with SOPs: Learn your role’s SOP and use it every time. If something is missing or confusing, tell us.

Role SOPs

Choose the SOP that matches your primary role. Many staff will read more than one so everyone understands how the whole pipeline fits together.

Events • Hosts & Above

Hosts & Event Leads

Plan events, gather Who / What / When / Where in Discord, and turn that into properly posted events across Notion, VRChat, Discord, and Fourthwall.

  • Own the event pipeline from idea to announcement.
  • Keep Notion’s Events database accurate.
  • Encourage post-event synopses & highlights.

Security • Moderation

Bouncers & Security

Handle in-world behavior, keep guests safe, and log every significant infraction so Admins have a clear, evidence-backed record to review.

  • Create clean, complete Notion case files.
  • Upload screenshots and evidence correctly.
  • Stay calm, neutral, and consistent.
Open Bouncer SOP Role focus: Safety & Documentation
Oversight • QA

Admins & Leadership

Review events and moderation cases in Notion, triage by severity, provide feedback to staff, and make sure the whole system runs smoothly.

  • Check that events & cases follow SOPs.
  • Mark severity (Critical / Non-severe) and status.
  • Close, follow up, or escalate as needed.

How to Use This Hub

  • New staff: Read this page once, then fully read your role SOP.
  • Cross-training: Hosts should know how Bouncers log cases, and Bouncers should know how events are planned.
  • Admins: Use this hub as the “front door” when updating SOPs or onboarding new team members.
  • Questions: If any SOP feels unclear, ping leadership in the staff channels so we can improve it.

SoraSkyClub runs on vibes, but it stays alive on structure. Thank you for helping keep the club organized, safe, and welcoming.
