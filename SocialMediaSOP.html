8. Handling Negative Comments, Harassment & Drama

Rule: Social media managers do not fight, dogpile, or ‚Äúratio‚Äù people. Your job is to protect the community, not win arguments.

  • Rude / spicy but non-hateful: Sarcasm, mild insults about the club/content.

  • Drama bait: Comments trying to drag SoraSkyClub into unrelated fights or calling out specific users/staff.

  • Do not over-explain or argue. Short, kind, done.

  • If you respond, keep it brief and neutral, or just ignore and move on.
  • If it starts escalating, stop replying and shift to moderation mode (see below).

8.3 Harassment, Hate & Safety Issues

  • Take screenshots of the comment/thread for internal records.
  • Immediately ping Mods/Admins in staff channels with:
    • Link to the post + comment.
    • Screenshot(s).
    • Short note: what happened, and if it targets specific people.
  • Do not quote-tweet, stitch, or otherwise signal-boost harassment.
  • Hide, delete, or report comments that:
    • Contain hate speech, threats, doxxing, or severe harassment.
  • Block accounts that are clearly malicious or repeatedly harassing.

8.4 Drama & Callout Threads

  • Do not respond from the SoraSkyClub account without leadership approval.
  • Take screenshots and links, share them with leadership and moderation.
  • Let leadership decide if:

    • We release a neutral statement.

  • Never encourage followers to ‚Äúgo after‚Äù someone or get involved in pile-ons.

9. Content Safety & Editing Guidelines

9.1 What Must Be Edited Out or Avoided

  • Hate symbols / extremist imagery: Anything tied to hate groups, Nazi/fascist imagery, or extremist movements should never appear in public SoraSkyClub content.

  • Unwanted/unknowing cameos: If someone is clearly the focus of a shot but never interacts with the club or may not want to be featured, pick another angle/clip.
