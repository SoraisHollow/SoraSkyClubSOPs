Social Media Manager SOP

This SOP explains how Social Media Managers keep SoraSkyClub visible, cozy, and safe across YouTube, TikTok, Twitter/X, Instagram, and Bluesky.

1. General Guidelines (All Platforms)

1.1 Brand Voice & Vibe

  • Tone: kind, playful, inclusive, slightly chaotic but never rude.
  • Keywords: cozy, neon, clouds, VRChat club, safe space, music, LGBTQ+ friendly.
  • Avoid public drama, callouts, or shaming individual members/staff.

1.2 Consent & Safety

  • Do not post identifiable people without:
    • their permission, or
    • a prior agreement for staff/performers being promoted.
  • Blur/crop out:
    • real names, personal info, Discord tags, or anything too revealing.
  • If someone requests content featuring them be removed:
    • Acknowledge, remove promptly, and notify leadership.

  • Reshare/RT performers and staff when they post about SoraSkyClub.
  • Do not argue in comments; if something gets heated, stop, screenshot, and ping leadership/mods.
